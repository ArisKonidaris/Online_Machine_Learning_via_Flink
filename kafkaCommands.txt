1) Get a quick-and-dirty single-node ZooKeeper instance:

> /home/aris/software/kafka/bin/zookeeper-server-start.sh /home/aris/software/kafka/config/zookeeper.properties


2) Start the Kafka server:

> /home/aris/software/kafka/bin/kafka-server-start.sh /home/aris/software/kafka/config/server.properties


3) Create topic named "psMessages" with a single partition and only one replica:

> /home/aris/software/kafka/bin/kafka-topics.sh --create --bootstrap-server localhost:9092 --replication-factor 1 --partitions 1 --topic psMessages


4) To list the topics:

> /home/aris/software/kafka/bin/kafka-topics.sh --list --bootstrap-server localhost:9092


5) For more info check https://kafka.apache.org/quickstart
